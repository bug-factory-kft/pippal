  Pro extension package (`pippal_pro`) that is **not** under this
  repository's MIT licence. That binary's terms are governed by
  the Microsoft Store EULA you accept on installation.
- Voice model files downloaded by the Voice Manager have their own

- Piper itself (`piper.exe`) is MIT-licensed but bundles eSpeak NG
  (GPL-3.0). That GPL boundary stays inside Piper's process; PipPal
